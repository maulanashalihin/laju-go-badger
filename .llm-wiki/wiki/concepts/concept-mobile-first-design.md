# Mobile-First Design Standards

Every page in Laju Go (SSR `.templ` or Inertia Svelte) must follow these design standards:

## Principles

1. **Visual Hierarchy** — Clear focal point; size/weight/contrast guides the eye
2. **Generous Whitespace** — Minimum 16-24px padding; 32-48px between sections
3. **Typography** — Bold/weighty headings, clean sans-serif body; loose letter-spacing for headlines
4. **Color** — 60-30-10 rule: neutral dominant, minimal accent. Avoid saturated colors unless requested
5. **Micro-interactions** — Hover states, smooth transitions, subtle shadows. No dead elements
6. **Mobile-first** — Responsive at 375px, 768px, and 1440px viewports
